# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard

Interactive sales analytics dashboard developed using Looker Studio to monitor sales performance, customer trends, regional contribution, and product category insights through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

## 🔗 Live Dashboard
👉 [View Interactive Dashboard](https://datastudio.google.com/reporting/2631d2cf-41ff-4aad-8b09-548c9e163a98)

## 📌 Dashboard Highlights
- Analyzed **2.53M Total Sales Amount** and **12.53K Quantity Sold**
- Tracked monthly sales performance from **Jul 2023 – Jan 2024**
- Compared sales contribution across:
  - Regions
  - Product Categories
  - Sales Representatives
  - Customer Types
- Evaluated customer segmentation between **New vs Returning Customers**
- Built interactive filters for dynamic business analysis

## 📈 Key Insights
- **North Region** recorded the highest sales performance
- **Returning Customers** contributed over 50% of total sales
- Peak sales were achieved during **October and November 2023**
- Clothing and Food categories generated strong regional performance
- David emerged as the top-performing sales representative

## 📂 Business Use Case
This dashboard enables businesses to monitor sales growth, analyze regional and category performance, identify top-performing sales representatives, and support strategic decision-making through interactive data visualization.
